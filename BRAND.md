## Future Upgrades (v2.0 — 98/100 Target)

### 1. Custom Glyph Design

**Current:** Unicode ∞8 (anyone can copy)
**Target:** Custom Möbius 8 loop with chain nodes

**Specs:**
- **Design:** Single continuous stroke forming infinity loop + 8
- **Style:** Monoline brutalist (consistent stroke width)
- **Details:**
  - One loop slightly larger (origin → future)
  - 2-3 micro "nodes" on path (provenance chain links)
  - Perfect geometric balance
- **Use cases:**
  - Logo (svg, png)
  - Favicon
  - Social media icons
  - Merch embroidery
  - NOT for typing (keep unicode ∞8 for text)

**Budget:** $200-500 (designer commission)
**Timeline:** 2-3 days design iteration
**When:** After product-market fit proven (100+ declarations created)

**Designer brief:**
> Create a custom geometric symbol combining infinity (∞) and the number 8 in a single continuous stroke. Style: Brutalist monoline (like Supreme box or Chanel interlocking Cs). Must work at tiny sizes (16px favicon) and massive scales (billboard). Include 2-3 subtle "nodes" along the path to represent provenance chain links. Black on white and white on black versions.

## Brand Voice & Positioning

### What ∞8 ARCH Is
- Architectural permanence for creative provenance
- Infrastructure for AI-native music economy
- Proof of process, not confession of assistance
- For masters-level producers, not consumers

### What ∞8 ARCH Is NOT
- Not a blockchain (uses IPFS, not crypto)
- Not surveillance (opt-in transparency)
- Not backward compatible with old industry (ISRC/ISWC)
- Not for listeners (infrastructure for makers)

### Tone
- **Confident**, not defensive
- **Philosophical**, not preachy
- **Technical**, not jargon-heavy
- **Provocative**, not alienating

### Example messaging
✅ "Using AI tools isn't shameful. Using them poorly is."
✅ "Process is provenance. Mastery is transparent."
✅ "Every track has infinite lineage. Build your chain."
❌ "Verify your authenticity" (defensive framing)
❌ "Blockchain-powered music metadata" (crypto jargon)
❌ "The future of music rights management" (boring)
